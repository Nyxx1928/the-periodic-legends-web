We teach Earth Science
differently here...

As students at STI College Caloocan, we're developing The Periodic Legends (TPL) to make Earth Science engaging for GAS students at Caloocan High School.

Through gamification and analytics, we aim to boost motivation and learning outcomes in resource-limited settings.

Led by strong values

Innovation in Education

We integrate game-based learning to transform traditional Earth Science teaching, fostering engagement and critical thinking.

Accessibility for All

Designed for resource-constrained environments like public schools, ensuring reliable access via mobile and web platforms.

Data-Driven Insights

Web-based analytics help teachers monitor progress and customize lessons for GAS students at Caloocan High School.

Student Growth Focus

Our RPG mechanics encourage problem-solving and retention of Earth Science concepts through interactive gameplay.

Curriculum Alignment

TPL is tailored to the Philippine K-12 Earth Science curriculum, integrating quizzes and combat systems seamlessly.

Community-Centric

Developed by BS Computer Science students to support fellow learners and teachers in our local community.
